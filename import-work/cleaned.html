Latest News

New route: Touching down in Thailand

31st October 2025

We're delighted to announce that from 18th October 2026, Virgin Atlantic will be soaring seasonally to Phuket, as we launch non-stop flights from London Heathrow to Phuket, Thailand. Our new route opens up fresh opportunities for our cargo customers, connecting our network to one of Southeast Asias most vibrant regions.

Find out more

Virgin Atlantic Board Announces New CEO

13th October 2025

The Virgin Atlantic Board is pleased to announce its succession plan for the airlines Chief Executive Officer, Shai Weiss. After a successful seven-year tenure, Shai will stand down as Chief Executive and leave the airline on 31 December 2025. Corneel Koster will succeed Shai and become CEO on 1 January 2026.

Find out more

New marketplace partnership: CargoAi

06th October 2025

Virgin Atlantic Cargo has announced a new strategic partnership with CargoAi, expanding its presence on digital marketplaces. This collaboration marks an important milestone in the companys ongoing commitment to delivering customer-centric, technology-driven solutions and enhancing the digital booking experience for its customers.
